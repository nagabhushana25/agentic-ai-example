# Databricks Use Cases

The Databricks Analyst Agent is intended for structured analytical tasks such as:

- querying curated warehouse tables
- analyzing sales or support trends
- validating KPI calculations
- checking ETL load status
- finding data quality issues
- investigating dashboard discrepancies
- measuring backlog impact on reporting timelines

Example questions for Databricks analysis:
- show net sales by region for the last 30 days
- find ETL jobs that failed in the last week
- calculate average ticket resolution time by support team
- identify customers with no purchases in the last 90 days
- summarize open backlog by team and priority
