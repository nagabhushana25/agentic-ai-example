# KPI Definitions

Important KPIs used by the business data assistant:

Daily Sales:
- total net sales for the current day

Monthly Revenue:
- total net sales aggregated for the current month

Customer Churn Rate:
- percentage of customers who became inactive during the measurement period

Average Order Value:
- net sales divided by number of completed orders

Ticket Resolution Time:
- average time between ticket creation and resolution

First Response Time:
- average time between ticket creation and first agent response

SLA Compliance:
- percentage of tickets resolved within SLA threshold

ETL Job Success Rate:
- percentage of ETL jobs completed successfully in the measurement period

Dashboard Refresh Latency:
- difference between current time and the latest successful dashboard dataset refresh

Active Customer Count:
- number of customers with at least one completed purchase in the last 90 days

Backlog Open Count:
- number of backlog work items still in todo or in_progress state

Critical Backlog Count:
- number of high-impact backlog items still unresolved
