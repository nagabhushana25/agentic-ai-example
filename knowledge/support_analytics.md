# Support Analytics

Support analytics focuses on ticket trends, SLA performance, and issue patterns.

Common ticket dimensions:
- priority
- category
- region
- support team
- product area
- channel

Common support metrics:
- open ticket count
- resolved ticket count
- average resolution time
- average first response time
- escalation rate
- SLA breach rate
- reopen rate

Business questions answered by support analytics:
- which categories generate the most high priority tickets
- which region has the highest SLA breach rate
- which support team resolves tickets fastest
- whether reopen rate increased after a product release
- which ETL or reporting issues are creating support volume
