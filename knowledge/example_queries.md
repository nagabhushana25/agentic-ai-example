# Example Business Questions

Examples of prompts the Business Data Assistant should handle:

- Explain the ETL pipeline for customer and order reporting
- What is the business logic for active customers
- How is net sales calculated
- Which KPIs are used in the executive dashboard
- What does SLA compliance mean in support analytics
- What data quality checks are applied before dashboard publishing
- Summarize the reporting layers used in this system
- Describe how support ticket metrics are calculated
- What are common Databricks use cases in this platform
- Explain the difference between gross sales and net sales
- Summarize backlog KPIs for analytics and data engineering teams
