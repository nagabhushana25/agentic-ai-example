# Sales Analytics

Sales analytics tracks revenue, order behavior, and customer purchase trends.

Core sales dimensions:
- customer
- product
- region
- sales channel
- order date
- promotion

Core sales metrics:
- gross sales
- net sales
- completed orders
- cancelled orders
- average order value
- repeat purchase rate
- refund amount

Common business questions:
- which region has the highest net sales
- what is the monthly revenue trend
- which products have the highest refund rate
- how many active customers made repeat purchases
- which orders are impacted by ETL backlog or reporting issues
